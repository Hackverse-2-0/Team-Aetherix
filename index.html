The Problems We Solve

The agricultural supply chain is broken. We're fixing it from both ends.

Farmer Problems

Middlemen eat 30-40%

Farmers receive only 60-70% of market price due to exploitative intermediaries.

No Price Transparency

Without real-time data, farmers sell cheap while markets sell high.

No AI Support

Zero access to modern advisory tools for crop diseases, weather, or demand.

Lack of Knowledge

Modern techniques and government schemes remain inaccessible to most farmers.

Buyer Problems

No Quality Assurance

Buyers can't verify produce quality before purchase, leading to losses.

No Traceability

Farm-to-fork journey is opaque no way to verify source or practices.

Delayed Supply Chain

Multiple intermediaries create bottlenecks, delays, and waste.

Unfair Pricing

Buyers pay inflated rates while farmers earn less a broken equilibrium.
